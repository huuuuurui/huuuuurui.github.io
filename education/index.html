master's course

I graduated from 东北师范大学 (NorthEast Normal University) in 2020. NENU is widely considered as a top-5 university in northeast reigon of China. I majored in Physical Geography and my academic supervisor was Prof. 许 嘉巍 （Xu Jiawei), a very famous expert whose research interests focus on global change, landscape of Changbai Mountain and vegetation communities succession.(https://js.nenu.edu.cn/teacher/index_fix.php). After three-years’ hard work, I finished my master’s thesis titled with “Study on the changing process of typical tundra ecosystem under the invasion of Veratrum nigrum” and earned a Master’s degree of Science.
